THE MAN
BEHIND
THE DECKS

DJ Mikey (Michael Turner) has been a fixture in Houston's nightlife scene for over a decade — from backyard cookouts to packed clubs, he brings the same relentless energy every single time.

Rooted in hip-hop and trap, Mikey's sets aren't playlists — they're journeys. He reads the crowd, builds the room, and keeps the floor locked in from the first drop to the last track.

Available for club nights, private events, corporate functions, and weddings. If you want a DJ who shows up and shows out, you know who to call.
